## Getting Started

### Sign Up as a Coach

1. **Go to the Sign Up page**
   - Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage or navigate to the sign-up page

2. **Select "Coach"**
   - On the sign-up form, select the "Coach" option (radio button)

3. **Fill in your information**
   - **Email**: Enter your email address
   - **Full Name**: Enter your first and last name
   - **Password**: Create a password (must be at least 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm

4. **Submit the form**
   - Click the "Create Account" button

5. **Verify your email**
   - Check your email inbox for a verification code
   - Enter the 6-digit code on the verification page
   - Click "Verify Email"
   - You'll be redirected to the sign-in page once verified

6. **Sign in**
   - Use your email and password to sign in
   - You'll now have access to the coach dashboard

---

### Sign Up as a Member/Client

**Option 1: Sign up with an invitation**
1. Check your email for an invitation from your coach
2. Click the invitation link
3. Fill in your information (email, full name, password)
4. Verify your email with the code sent to you
5. You're ready to join sessions!

**Option 2: Direct sign up**
1. Go to the Sign Up page
2. Select "Member" option
3. Fill in your information
4. Verify your email
5. Sign in to access your profile

---

### Sign In

1. Go to the Sign In page
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard

---

### Forgot Password

1. On the Sign In page, click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Code"
4. Check your email for a 6-digit code
5. Enter the code and your new password
6. Click "Reset Password"
7. Sign in with your new password

## Coach Features

### Adding Clients

1. **Navigate to Clients**
   - Click "Clients" in the navigation menu (left sidebar)

2. **Add a new client**
   - Click the "Add New Client" button (usually at the top right)
   - A panel will open on the right side

3. **Fill in client information**
   - **Name**: Enter the client's full name
   - **First Name**: Enter first name
   - **Last Name**: Enter last name
   - **Email**: Enter email address (required for sending invitations)
   - **Sport**: Select the sport type from the dropdown
   - **Notes**: Add any additional notes (optional)

4. **Save the client**
   - Click "Add Client" button
   - The client will be added to your client list
   - You can now schedule sessions for this client

---

### Scheduling Sessions

1. **Go to the Schedule page**
   - Click "Schedule" in the navigation menu
   - You'll see a calendar view

2. **Click "Schedule" button**
   - Look for the "Schedule" button (usually at the top right with a "+" icon)

3. **Fill in session details**
   - **Title**: Enter a name for the session (e.g., "John's Training Session")
   - **Date**: Select the date using the date picker
   - **Time**: Select the time from the dropdown
   - **Duration**: Choose how long the session will last (in minutes)
   - **Session Type**: Choose one of:
     - **Virtual 1:1 Session**: One-on-one video session
     - **Virtual Group Session**: Multiple participants in one session
     - **In-Person 1:1 Motion Capture**: In-person session with motion capture
   - **Client**: Select a client from the dropdown (or click "Add New Client" to add one first)
   - **Notes**: Add any session notes (optional)

4. **Create the session**
   - Click "Create Session" button
   - The session will appear on your calendar

---

### Managing Sessions

1. **View your sessions**
   - Go to the Schedule page
   - You'll see all your sessions on the calendar
   - Click on any session to view details

2. **View session details**
   - Click on a session in the calendar
   - A panel will open showing:
     - Session title and date/time
     - Participants
     - Session link (for sharing)
     - Notes

3. **Invite additional participants** (for group sessions)
   - Open a session's details
   - Click "Invite More" button next to Participants
   - Select clients from the list
   - Click "Send Invitations"
   - Invitations will be sent via email

4. **Copy session link**
   - In the session details panel, find the "Session Link" section
   - Click the copy icon to copy the link
   - Share this link with participants so they can join

---

### Viewing AI Insights

1. **Join a session**
   - Start or join a video session (see [Joining a Session](#joining-a-session))

2. **Open AI Insights tab**
   - In the video session, you'll see two tabs: "Session" and "AI Insights"
   - Click on the "AI Insights" tab

3. **View movement analysis**
   - The AI will automatically analyze movement every 30 seconds
   - You'll see insights including:
     - Movement quality and patterns
     - Posture metrics
     - Performance interpretation
     - Risk levels
     - Targeted recommendations

4. **For multiple participants**
   - If there are multiple participants, insights will be organized in an accordion
   - Click on a participant's name to expand their insights

---

### Exporting Session Summaries

1. **After a session ends**
   - Go to the Schedule page
   - Click on a completed session (or one that has ended)

2. **Download summary**
   - In the session details panel, you'll see a "Download Session Summary Report" button
   - Click this button
   - A PDF report will be downloaded to your computer
   - The report includes:
     - All AI insights from the session
     - Summary of movement analysis
     - Key findings and recommendations
     - Performance metrics

**Note**: The summary is generated using AI and combines all insights from the session into a comprehensive report.

---

### Ending Sessions

1. **During a video session**
   - Join the session as the coach
   - Scroll down in the "Session" tab
   - You'll see a red "End Session" button at the bottom

2. **End the session**
   - Click "End Session"
   - Confirm that you want to end the session
   - The session will be marked as completed
   - No one will be able to join after this
   - You'll be redirected to the schedule page

**Important**: Only coaches can end sessions. Once ended, participants cannot join.

## Member/Client Features

### Joining Sessions

1. **Receive an invitation**
   - Check your email for a session invitation from your coach
   - The email will contain a "Join Session" button or link

2. **Click the session link**
   - Click the link in the email
   - Or copy and paste the session link into your browser

3. **Sign in (if needed)**
   - If you're not signed in, you'll be asked to sign in first
   - Use your email and password

4. **Join the video session**
   - Once signed in, you'll automatically join the video session
   - Allow camera and microphone permissions when prompted

---

### Viewing Your Profile

1. **Go to your profile**
   - Click on your name or profile icon in the navigation
   - Select "Profile" from the menu

2. **View your information**
   - You can see your profile details
   - View your assigned sessions
   - Check your progress

## Video Sessions

### Joining a Session

1. **Get the session link**
   - Receive it via email from your coach, or
   - Get it from the session details if you're the coach

2. **Click the link**
   - The link will take you to the session page
   - Sign in if prompted

3. **Allow permissions**
   - Your browser will ask for camera and microphone access
   - Click "Allow" to enable video and audio

4. **You're in!**
   - You'll see yourself and other participants
   - The session has started

---

### Video Controls

At the bottom of the video session, you'll see control buttons:

- **Microphone**: Click to mute/unmute your microphone
- **Camera**: Click to turn your camera on/off
- **Screen Share**: Click to share your screen (coaches only)
- **Leave**: Click to exit the session

---

### Layout Options

1. **Grid Layout**
   - Click the grid icon in the top center
   - Shows all participants in a grid view
   - Good for seeing everyone at once

2. **Spotlight Layout** (Focus Mode)
   - Click the person icon in the top center
   - Shows one participant in the main view
   - Other participants appear in a smaller carousel on the side
   - Click on a participant in the carousel to spotlight them

**Note**: For 1:1 sessions, the participant will be in the main view and the coach in the small view.

---

### AI Movement Analysis

1. **Automatic analysis**
   - The AI automatically analyzes movement every 30 seconds
   - No action needed from you

2. **View insights**
   - Click the "AI Insights" tab during the session
   - You'll see real-time movement analysis
   - Insights update automatically

3. **What the AI analyzes**
   - Movement quality and patterns
   - Posture and alignment
   - Balance and symmetry
   - Risk factors
   - Performance recommendations

**Note**: For in-person motion capture sessions, the coach points the camera at the athlete, and the AI analyzes the athlete's movement (the athlete doesn't need to be in the video session).

## Troubleshooting

### Can't sign in?
- Make sure you've verified your email
- Check that you're using the correct email and password
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset your password

### Can't join a session?
- Make sure the session hasn't ended (check with your coach)
- Verify you're using the correct session link
- Make sure you're signed in
- Check that your browser allows camera/microphone access

### Video not working?
- Check your browser permissions for camera and microphone
- Make sure no other application is using your camera
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection

### Can't see AI insights?
- Make sure you're in an active video session
- Wait 30 seconds for the first analysis to appear
- Check the "AI Insights" tab (not the "Session" tab)
- For mocap sessions, make sure the coach's camera is pointing at the athlete

### PDF export not working?
- Make sure the session has ended or has insights
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments
- Contact support if the issue persists

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Use Chrome or Firefox** for the best video quality
2. **Good lighting** helps the AI analyze movement better
3. **Stable internet connection** ensures smooth video
4. **Allow camera permissions** when prompted
5. **For coaches**: Point camera at the athlete during mocap sessions for accurate analysis
